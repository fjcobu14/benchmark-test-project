# Dealership Expansion Report: Austin to San Antonio

## Executive Summary

This brief assesses the viability of opening a second dealership location in San Antonio, TX, based on three key factors: the Austin location's July 2022 financial performance, the competitive landscape near Austin city center, and the geographic proximity between the two cities.

---

## 1. July 2022 Expense Profile (Austin Location)

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Highest Expense Type** | Employee Salaries |
| **Net Profit** | $374,170.51 |
| Total Sales | $436,652.79 |
| Total Expenses | $62,482.28 |
| Total Units Sold | 191 |
| Best Selling Category | Sedan |
| Total Employees | 24 |

**Key Takeaway:** Employee Salaries represented the largest expense category in July 2022. Despite this, the dealership achieved a strong net profit of $374,170.51, indicating healthy margins and operational efficiency. The expense-to-sales ratio was approximately 14.3%, suggesting well-controlled costs relative to revenue.

**Note:** Peddle (rating 4.5) was found at essentially the same coordinates as the subject dealership (~0.12 km away at 3800 N Lamar Blvd, Suite 230) but is an online car-buying service rather than a traditional dealership, and is therefore not considered a true competitor.

**Key Takeaway:** The immediate competitive zone around Austin city center is relatively sparse for traditional car dealerships. Dynamic Motors, Inc is the highest-rated true competitor within 5 km, with a 4.4 rating. This suggests limited direct competition in the downtown core, which may be favorable for the current location but also indicates that dealership density increases along the I-35 corridor further from the center.

| **Estimated Drive Time** | ~1 hour 22 minutes |

**Key Takeaway:** The 128.2 km driving distance via I-35 S makes San Antonio a practical same-day travel destination for management, logistics, and customer overlap analysis. The relatively short distance supports the feasibility of shared operational resources (e.g., vehicle transfers, regional marketing campaigns, management oversight) between the two locations.

---

## Conclusion

The Austin dealership demonstrated strong profitability in July 2022 with a net profit of $374,170.51, despite Employee Salaries being the highest expense category. The competitive landscape near the Austin city center is manageable, with the best-rated true competitor (Dynamic Motors, Inc) scoring only 4.4/5 at ~1.6 km away. The 128.2 km driving distance to San Antonio via I-35 S supports operational connectivity between the two locations. These factors collectively suggest favorable conditions for expansion, provided that San Antonio's local market demand and competitive environment are separately validated.
