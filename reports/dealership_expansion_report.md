## Summary & Recommendation Considerations

- The Austin dealership posted a robust **$374,170.51 net profit** in July 2022, with **Employee Salaries** as the dominant expense — suggesting scalability if labor efficiencies can be replicated at a second site.
- The immediate competitive pressure near the Austin location is modest, with the best-rated nearby competitor (Peddle, 4.5 stars) being a niche online car-buying service rather than a full-line traditional dealership.
- The **128 km** distance to San Antonio supports operational connectivity (regional advertising, inventory sharing, management oversight) while tapping a distinct metropolitan market of ~1.5 million residents.

Management should weigh these financial and competitive factors alongside San Antonio market demand, real estate costs, and staffing requirements before proceeding with the expansion.
