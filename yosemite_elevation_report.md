# Yosemite Campground Elevation Safety Assessment

## Summary
Based on local research notes, the 10 documented Yosemite campgrounds have an average elevation of 6,130 ft.

### Key Findings
- Highest elevation: Tuolumne Horse Campsites at 8,600 ft
- Lowest elevation: Camp 4 / Lower Pines / North Pines at 4,000 ft
- High elevation campgrounds (7,000+ ft): 4 of 10 documented
- Air quality: US EPA Index 1 (Good), PM2.5: 2.1 ug/m3
